2022 ExtraordinaryIS Scholarships

The Otis and Rosie Brown Foundation is now accepting scholarship applications for class of 2022 high school seniors in the North Texas area. Applications will be accepted until the end of April, 2022.

The foundation is also accepting applications for sustaining grants from previous ExtraordinaryIS scholarship awardees.
